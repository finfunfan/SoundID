How the audio works — pick a region and the pool is every species in the catalogue for that continent (filter by family if you want to drill one group). Recordings are pulled live from Xeno-canto's Creative-Commons collection via GBIF's open API — no key — and stream on demand into an <audio> element, so nothing is stored on your device. Only CC-licensed clips are used, with recordist and licence shown. If a species has no fetchable clip, a short synthesised tone stands in so the quiz never stalls.
